COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING CHENGANNUR

VISION : To emerge as an institution of excellence that imparts knowledge through education, learning and reasearch for the inclusive development of society. MISSION : To impart engineering education that enables the students to design, develop, and maintain products and process for the society utilizing the evolving technology.

ABOUT US


The College of Engineering(CEC), was established by the government of Kerala under the aegis of IHRD, in the year 1993. It has been approved by All India Council for Tehnical Education(AICTE) and affiliated to APJ ABDUL KALAM Technological University (KTU). Ever since its inception, the college has maintained its eminence as a leading engineering college in Kerala. Being located in Chengannur Town, Alappuzha District, the college has got access to all means of transport,communication and lodging facilities.
