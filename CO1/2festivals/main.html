FESTIVALS OF INDIA




India is a land of diverse cultures and religions, which leads to a multitude of festivals celebrated throughout the year. Some prominent festivals include Diwali (Festival of Lights), Holi (Festival of Colors), Eid, Christmas, Navratri, Durga Puja, and Makar Sankranti. These festivals reflect India's rich heritage and vibrant traditions, celebrated with music, dance, food, and religious rituals.
